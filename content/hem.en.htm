Welcome to Tyresö Women and Girls shelter

Women are always welcome to contact us for help, support and sheltered homes. We do not have any connection with authorities and you will stay anonymous. The information you share with us is confidential which means that we do not tell anyone about your experiences or your identity unless you want us to.

If you want to contact us now you can call 08-742 23 33.


Clear browsing history

Your browser saves the website you have visited. You can delete the history if you do not want anyone to find this information. Press on the icon of the web browser you use and you will find a step by step guide on how to delete your history.

A button with the text “Leave” will always be visual when you visit our website. If you have to redirect to another website quickly, press the Leave button and it will redirect you to Google’s start page.

Remember that the Leave button does not clear your browsing history
