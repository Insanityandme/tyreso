About us

Tyresö women’s shelter is a feministic non-profit organization that is politically and religiously independent. We want to help, support and offer a sheltered home for women who lives with threats and abuse.

We also run Tjejjouren where we focus on younger girls. We arrange special evenings where young girls can meet, talk and have fun with each other. We also answer questions they might have in our chat and e-mail. If you want to know more, go to http://tyreso.tjejjouren.se/.
