Take notes

every time he hits you. It will make it easier for you to go back and remember what happened.

favorite

Talk to someone.

Let someone know what is happening to you and what you are going through. It can be a friend, relative or someone at work. You don’t have to be ready to leave him, but it is good if another person knows what you are going through.

smartphone

Document your injuries.

Write down or photograph your injuries. When you visit the doctor you can demand that he take pictures. It is good to have pictures if you decide to report him in the future, even if you don’t want to leave him now.

warning

Don’t believe him when he says he is going to stop hitting you.

Find out who you can turn to, even if something happens in the middle of the night. It can be social services, a friend or a relative. Ask friends or relatives for a key.
